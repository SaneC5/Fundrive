To rent a car as an Indian citizen, you will need to provide the following documents:
1. Valid Indian Driver’s License: Issued in your state of residence.
2. Credit Card: In your name, to cover the security deposit and payments.
3. Government-Issued Photo ID: This can include Aadhar card, Voter ID, or Passport.
4. Proof of Address: Required if the address on your ID is outdated or unclear.

If you are a foreign national, you will need the following documents to rent a car in India:
1. International Driver’s License (IDP): If your home country’s license is not in English or not recognized in India.
2. Passport and Valid Visa: A passport with a tourist visa or Indian visa is required.
3. Credit Card: Must be in your name for payment and security deposit.
4. Indian Driver’s License (Optional): If you plan to stay in India for more than a year, you may need to get an Indian Driver’s License.
